About me

Masters of Science student at American University in Washington, D.C., pursuing an M.S. in Data Science with an expected graduation date of May 2026. My academic background combines statistical rigor with applied analytics, and my completed coursework includes Statistical Methods, Regression, Statistical Programming in R, and Business Insights/Analytics.
